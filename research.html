FinTech vs Non-FinTech M&A Takeover Premiums (2010–2024)

This research examines whether fintech firms command higher takeover premiums compared to non-fintech firms between 2010 and 2024.

Using M&A transaction data, the study analyses valuation patterns, strategic acquisition behaviour, and how digital finance innovation impacts investor expectations.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Takeover premium comparison
  • Valuation dynamics in fintech
  • Strategic M&A behaviour in digital finance
  • Market perception of fintech growth models

Methodology:

Quantitative regression analysis on cross-industry M&A transactions from 2010–2024.
